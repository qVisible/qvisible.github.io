Business with Digital Marketing

Hi everyone! Owen from the Digital Marketing Stream here. I briefly met you early in 2020 but this is another check in again about the Digital Marketing Stream that is available to you all starting 3rd year business. If you choose this stream you will graduate with a "Business with Digital Marketing" Degree.

Below is the subject differences that students who choose the Digital Marketing stream will experience and give you a flavour of the type of work you would be doing.

In summary, following the Digital Marketing stream will give you more skills in the online and screen marketing space. It will highlight your ability to not only create the design and strategy of digital marketing campaigns but also lead the creation of the visual and web assets themselves.

If you have any questions about the Digital Marketing stream please email me at bradyo@itcarlow.ie. I'd be delighted to talk to you!
Year 3

The big subject difference in year 3 is that Digital Marketing Students will take a subject called "Digital Media Design" (This will be instead of Financial Management subject). This year the subject is taught by Patrick Morgan, Course Director of Visual Communications & Design degree.

In the Digital Media Design subject you'll explore visual design and how it combines with marketing to influence customers perceptions of brands and products. You will also discover the main software tools of design including Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator

See the media above for some samples of past student's work. These are all design and branding solutions created by students in response to assignments in the subject.

Your 3rd year work placement will also be in an digital marketing environment.

Just like the general Business stream you can exit after Year 3 with a level 7 degree or continue onto Year 4.
Year 4

In year 4 you take the subject "Web Design Methods"(Instead of Corporate Finance).

This module is all about learning the technologies (html, css, javascript) to design and develop websites.

Instead of completing a business thesis you will carry out a digital marketing project. This will be similar to a thesis but will focus on an aspect of Digital Marketing and there will be some practical outputs of marketing design from the process.
